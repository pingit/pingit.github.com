Frequently Asked Questions

What are cash programs and sweep options?

E*TRADE Securities ("E*TRADE") makes available to its customers a number of programs designed to handle uninvested cash in their brokerage accounts. Uninvested cash may exist in an account for any number of reasons, including receipt of dividends on a security or proceeds from the sale of a security. Some of these cash programs have a sweep feature, which automatically transfers ("sweeps") the uninvested cash into a program such as the ESDA or the JPM MMF (ticker symbol JLEXX). Please see the enclosed chart showing all the options that may be available to you.

What sweep option is being offered to me now?

You are being offered the JPM MMF, a money market mutual fund that seeks current income with liquidity and stability of principal by investing in high-quality, short-term money market instruments. You should carefully consider the investment objectives, risks, charges and expenses of the Fund in the enclosed Summary Prospectus before making your decision. The full prospectus and the Statement of Additional Information are available at www.etrade.com/prospectus and can also be provided at no cost by calling 1-800-387-2331.

When will this change occur?

Your sweep option will be changed to the JPM MMF on or around January 2, 2013.

What are the main differences between the ESDA and JPM MMF?

The ESDA is a bank deposit account insured by FDIC for up to $1.25 million (to learn more about FDIC insurance, please visit www.fdic.gov) whereas the JPM MMF is a money market mutual fund protected by SIPC for up to $500,000 (to learn more about SIPC protection, please visit www.sipc.org). The 7-day yield for the Fund as of market close on 11/9/12 was 0.05% compared to the interest rate of 0.01% for the ESDA. The Fund’s investment adviser, administrator, and distributor have contractually agreed to waive certain fees and/or reimburse certain expenses under certain circumstances until 7/1/13, at which time these service providers will determine whether or not to renew or revise the waiver. As of 11/9/12, the 7-day yield without the expense waivers was -0.76%. Although the Fund is not affiliated with E*TRADE or its affiliates, the Fund pays affiliates of E*TRADE for the sale of Fund shares and related services.

How does the sweep into the JPM MMF work?

Any uninvested cash in your brokerage account will be automatically transferred to buy shares of the JPM MMF, which will be shown on your account statement with a cash value associated with it. The JPM MMF will accrue dividends nightly that will be reinvested monthly in Fund shares. Your brokerage statement will reflect the JPM MMF account balances and all transaction activities (deposits and withdrawals) for the statement period.

When cash is needed in your brokerage account (such as to purchase a security, pay fees, cover checks you have written on your brokerage account, or if you wish to withdraw money from your brokerage account), the amount will be transferred automatically from your JPM MMF account back to your brokerage account.

Can I still write checks and use my ATM/VISA® Debit Card on my brokerage account?

Yes. You will continue to enjoy all the cash management features of your brokerage account as you do with the ESDA. If you currently have check-writing and/or ATM/VISA® Debit Card privileges for your brokerage account, you can continue to use them. Available funds will be automatically transferred from your JPM MMF account to your brokerage account to cover any transactions you make.

Is there a limit on the number of transfers between my JPM MMF account and my E*TRADE Securities account?

No. An unlimited number of automatic sweep transfers are permitted. _ _ How can I check my JPM MMF balance?

You may view your balance online any time by logging into your account. In addition, your account statement will show the JPM MMF period-end balance.

Who can I contact if I have more questions?

If you have any questions about how the sweep program works, the JPM MMF, or if you wish to remain in the ESDA, you may contact us at 1-800-387-2331 between 7 a.m. and midnight ET, any day of the week.
